About Crafty Cuddles

Handmade with Passion, Crafted with Love ❤️

Crafty Cuddles is a creative handmade art brand founded by Anika Sharma, driven by a passion for turning imagination into beautiful handcrafted creations.

From vibrant paintings and elegant bookmarks to unique crochet pieces and personalized gifts, every product is carefully designed to bring happiness, creativity, and a personal touch into people's lives.

What started as a creative hobby has grown into a mission to celebrate art, craftsmanship, and meaningful gifting. Every creation reflects dedication, attention to detail, and the joy of making something truly special.

Meet The Founder

Anika Sharma believes that handmade art carries emotions that mass-produced products cannot. Through Crafty Cuddles, she aims to create products that tell stories, spread smiles, and make every moment a little more memorable.
